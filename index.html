Past Work

What

Zero Proof is a recipe site for non-alcoholic mixed drinks, otherwise known as virgin cocktails or mocktails.

Design

In response to recipes hosted on text-filled blogs and protracted listicles, Zero Proof has been designed to be easy to use with minimal clutter. This way, people can spend less time reading, and more time enjoying their drink.

1 / 4
A grid layout makes it easy to browse through recipes and quickly find something interesting.
2 / 4
The individual recipe pages are uncluttered and get right to the recipe.
3 / 4
Responsive layouts are designed with mobile use in mind.
4 / 4
All the info you need is visible at once; it's more fun to mix drinks when you don't have to switch between pouring and scrolling.

What

Cascade Stone Supply is a concept site rebrand for a quarrying and stone supply company.

Design

This redesign has responsiveness and easy access as top priorities. This was achieved by using the BootStrap plugin to create easy to use layouts on any screen size, with a simple color scheme and site layout for ease of use.
